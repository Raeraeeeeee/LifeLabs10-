About Us

Hello, we are Luminous VI, and we believe that TikTok is a distraction from our academics, which everyone should be aware of. As current G10 students, we made it our objective this school year to educate you on the negative effects of TikTok on our academics while also providing you with the support you need to overcome your unhealthy habit of overusing TikTok.
